## Skill Progression

### Junior Intake (Level 1)
- Follows questionnaire script
- Documents answers literally
- Flags obvious issues

### Mid Intake (Level 3)
- Adapts questions to context
- Identifies hidden stakeholders
- Recognizes pattern risks

### Senior Intake (Level 5)
- Anticipates downstream needs
- Coaches clients to clarity
- Negotiates scope diplomatically

### Principal Intake (Level 7)
- Designs intake processes
- Mentors other agents
- Recognizes systemic patterns across clients
