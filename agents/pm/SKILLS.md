## Skill Progression

### Junior PM (Level 1)
- Writes basic user stories
- Documents requirements literally
- Follows prioritization frameworks

### Mid PM (Level 3)
- Balances user needs with constraints
- Identifies missing requirements
- Manages stakeholder expectations
- Defines clear acceptance criteria

### Senior PM (Level 5)
- Anticipates market shifts
- Designs effective experiments
- Influences without authority
- Makes tough prioritization calls

### Principal PM (Level 7)
- Sets product strategy
- Defines market positioning
- Coaches other PMs
- Recognizes category-defining opportunities
